I have this at least 4 times a week!

Might sound dry but just add some sauce!

Ingredients:

  • Chicken thighs
  • Broccoli
  • Rice of your choice
  1. Rinse rice etc... You know how to cook rice, right?
  2. Heat up a frying pan to high heat, add fat of choice, fry chicken thighs until cooked through
  3. Cook broccoli for maybe 5 minutes?
  4. Add sauce of choice. Mixing regular mayonnaise with sweet chilli sauce is pretty tasty btw..
