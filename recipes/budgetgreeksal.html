Greeksallad, kind of...

This is a lazy take on the Greeksallad.

  • Kalamata olives
  • Sallad of choice
  • Cucumba
  • Tomato
  • Olive oil, salt and black pepper
  1. Get a nice big sallad bowl
  2. Add sallad
  3. Add cucumba and tomato, sliced, diced or the whole thing
  4. Season with a drizzle of olive oil, salt and black pepper
